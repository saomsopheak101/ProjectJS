Pet Day Care

Our pet day care service includes supervised playtime, socialization with other pets, feeding, bathroom breaks, and plenty of cuddles and attention from our trained staff.

We offer pet day care from Monday to Friday, 7 AM to 7 PM. We also have limited hours on weekends for special occasions.

Yes, we encourage potential clients to schedule a tour of our facility to meet our staff and see the environment where your pet will be cared for.

Pet Grooming

Our grooming services include bathing, brushing, nail trimming, ear cleaning, and haircuts. We cater to all breeds and sizes, ensuring your pet looks and feels their best.

Grooming frequency depends on your pet’s breed and coat type. Generally, long-haired breeds may require grooming every 4-6 weeks, while short-haired breeds may only need it every 8-12 weeks.
